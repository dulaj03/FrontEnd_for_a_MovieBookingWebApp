ABOUT US

ABC Cinema is a new and innovative company that deals in providing clients with modern entertainment services in watching movies. The usage of up-to-date screening equipment, comfortable chairs and the variety of the formats provide a great opportunity to attract the audiences of different taste. Specializing in premium halls and cutting edge sound and vision, ABC Cinema’s mission statement is all about making movie magic. The goal of Theathers is to offer a unique and exceptional experience of watching movies combining comfort, quality and state-of-art equipment. They have made progressive transformations as the company’s primary goal is to offer the best in the film exhibition business to the customers in Sri Lanka.
